Cerbere App

Cerbere App is a web application to assist companies managing security authorizations, accreditations and documents for their employees. It's primarily aimed at security companies that need to cope with a lot of certifications and frequent renewal of security-related documents.
Quickly view the status of your employees, renew documents efficiently and stay productive for your business.
